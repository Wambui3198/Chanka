Homemade Crochet and Knitted items

Our features include:

Order

First, you should place an order two weeks prior. This gives us time to crochet or knit the item. You are also required to give us correct measurement which can be done using the guide in this link

Payment

A downpayment for the item should be made after placing the order. The rest of the payment can be made upon delivery or pick-up of the item after completion.

Delivery

A notification email will be sent when the item is ready for collection. It can be delivered to you in or around Nairobi(delivery charges are applied). Alternatively, it can be picked up at our drop-off points.
